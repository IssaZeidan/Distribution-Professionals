About Us


Jordan Ice & Aerated Water Company (JICE) "Pepsi" was incorporated in Jordan on 2/4/1968, as a limited liability company. At that time, it was owned by individuals and acquired a franchise by PepsiCo International for the production and packaging of soft drinks Pepsi, Miranda, and 7up.

The company was developed from a local company to a global company. This happened after the global company acquired full shares in the company in 1998, when it began to develop and expand its activities and transferred it from a carbonated soft drinks company to a comprehensive beverages company that produces soft drinks (Pepsi, Miranda, Seven Up, Mountain Dew, Shani), and Non-carbonated products such as (Lipton Ice Tea, Barrio non-alcoholic malt beverage and Frutz) in addition to the production of Aquafina water. all products have international specifications in quality and taste, where the latest international filtration systems are used in production and packaging.
